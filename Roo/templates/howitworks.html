How The Milkman Works:

Start by logging in using your Facebook account. Don't worry we won't post anything to your Facebook or use your information in anyway other than validating you are a real person!

Next we'll verify that you are part of the Princeton network, so just sign in using your Princeton netid and password

Next enter your Frist mailbox number. We just want to have your mailbox number stored for any shipments we need to make.

You'll have access to our home page, from which you can navigate to anywhere on the site.

Go to the All Crates page and you can search for any crates in our system by name, or shipping amounts.

If you go to the My Crates page, you'll see that you have no crates.

But you can add to crates simply by selecting a store and entering the necessary information.

The My Networks page will display the networks which you are apart of, which for the time being, is just Princeton.

And once you login you can find out even more about The Milkman by visiting the About page!

So login! And Happy Shopping!
